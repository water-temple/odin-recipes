Oatmeal

Description

My favorite food

Ingredients

  • 0.5 cup oatmeal
  • Some water
  • 0.5 banana, sliced
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter

Steps

  1. Put the oatmeal in a bowl.
  2. Put water in.
  3. Put the oatmeal in the microwave and microwave for 65 seconds.
  4. Slice the banana.
  5. Put the banana and peanut butter in the oatmeal.
